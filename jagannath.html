Shri Jagannath

The Jagannath Temple is an important Hindu temple dedicated to Jagannath, a form of Vishnu - one of the trinity of supreme divinity in Hinduism.The temple is sacred to all Hindus, and especially in those of the Vaishnava traditions.Jagannath, Balabhadra and Subhadra are a trio of deities worshipped at the temple. Puri is in the state of Odisha, on the eastern coast of India. King Indradyumna of Avanti has built the main temple of Jagannath at Puri.

It is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites.The puri temple is also famous because many legends believe that Krishna's heart was placed there and the material that it is made from damages the heart so they have to change it every twelve years. In Akshaya Tritiya every year the Chandan Yatra festival marks the commencement of the construction of the Chariots of the Rath Yatra.

Devotional Importance :

Jagannath, Balabhadra and Subhadra are a trio of deities worshipped at the temple. The inner sanctum of the temple contains the deities of them carved from sacred neem logs known as daru sitting on the bejewelled platform or ratnabedi, along with deities of Sudarshana Chakra, Madanmohan, Sridevi and Vishwadhatri.The deities are adorned with different clothing and jewels according to the season. Worship of these deities pre-dates the building of the temple and may have originated in an ancient tribal shrine.

Suitable time to visit:

Having a more or less pleasant climate throughout the year, Jagannath Puri is thronged by people all year round except for peak summers (April-June). The ideal months to take a trip to Puri are between October and February when neither the scorching heat nor the high humidity is present to cause any hindrance to your outdoor plans.

Location:

Puri located at the east and is situated in the state of Odisha which is one of the eastern states of India.

How to reach?:

The nearest airport to Jagannath Temple is Bhubaneswar (BBI) almost 50 km from the temple.

The distance between Puri Railway Station to Jagannath Temple Puri is 3 Km by road.
Railway Station Code: Puri (PURI)
